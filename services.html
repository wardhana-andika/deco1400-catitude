Counseling

We provide cat counseling for our customers. Everyone can call our office or ask something via or website for quick advice or make an appointment for a more comprehensive service.

For new cat owners, we have various methods to help you get to know better and discover your cat's behaviours and personality.

If your cat is misbehaving in any way, we can help you understand and change your cat's behaviour so you and your cat can live happily ever after.

Cat School

Our "kitty-garten" service can help you train your cats while you are busy during the day or if you cannot leave your cat alone at home.

The cat training that we will provide will be based on your thoughts or complaints about the cat. In general we will help improve your cat's behaviour.

If you cannot tend to your cat for some time or need someone to look after him/her, our clinic also provides day or overnight cat care services.

Dietician

If your cat is struggling with his or her weight, we can help you plan a suitable diet for your cat.

Cats at different age and different size needs different amounts of calories per day. For example, kittens need far more food than adult cats because they are still growing up.

We're also able to perform general checkup on your cat and see if your cat is missing some important nutrients in his or her diet. A balanced diet is very important to help your cat happy and healthy!

House Calls

If you cannot visit our clinic, we also provide house calls. You can ask us questions by calling us or sending a question via this website.

We can provide quick answers by phone or email if you are having minor problems or questions. For something requiring more care, our staff can visit your house and perform necessary steps to mend your cat's problem.

Our main area of expertise is in cat psychology and behaviour, more or less just trying to work out the differences between owner and pet and try to make a healthy household for everyone
